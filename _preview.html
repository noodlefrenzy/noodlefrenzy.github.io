About

I'm Mike Lanzetta — a Principal Architect at Microsoft ISE based in Tokyo, where I lead data science discipline for North Asia. I work with enterprise customers to design and ship AI-native systems, from classical ML to agentic architectures.

After 20+ years shipping software at Amazon, Microsoft, and startups, I started writing about the fundamental shift happening in how engineering teams work with AI. The result is Hypervelocity Engineering — a framework my team developed for integrating AI into the engineering inner loop without sacrificing rigor.

This site and my Substack document what I'm learning along the way.
